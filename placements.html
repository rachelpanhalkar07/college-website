Process and Support

Placement Details

The placement process is coordinated through structured company registrations, pre-placement talks, aptitude assessments, technical interviews, and final HR discussions. Students are guided throughout the cycle with timely communication and preparation support.

The Career Development Cell conducts training in resume building, aptitude practice, group discussions, coding readiness, mock interviews, and professional communication. Industry interaction, alumni mentoring, and internship opportunities help students build confidence before final placements.
